About us

Mg Foundation“Service of oppressed humanity is service of the almighty” Established in 2010, Mg Foundation is the Top Nonprofit Trust headquartered in Udaipur, Rajasthan India

The organization started 3 decades ago with a vision to serve the needy and cure the helpless patients through free corrective surgeries with a will to fight a battle with Polio and other related birth disabilities. An internationally renowned center with more than 12 specialized hospitals, 1100+ beds, food for 4500+ people every day, and more than 427850 free of cost corrective surgeries; the Foundation has become more like a family and home to all the patients and their visitors.
